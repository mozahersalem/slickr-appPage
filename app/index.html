Welcome To Slickr APP

The Outfit Builder is the most engaging fashion discovery engine to create, share and shop your favorite outfits from all top fashion brands around the globe.

Thanks to its advanced recommendation engine, The Outfit Builder makes it easier to swipe through items matching your search and fastest to find the right fit for you.

You can seek out inspiration by browsing through the categories such as a style, brand or color. The Outfit Builder allows you to create, save, share and shop endless combinations of clothes in the most friendly user interface.
